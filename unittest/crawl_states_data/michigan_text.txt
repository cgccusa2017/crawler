Bedrock Transformational Sites – Underwriting Analysis

Revised Date: 
May 18, 2018
Transformational Brownfield Plans (TBP) proposing to use more than $1.5 million in annual income and withholding tax capture revenues require an independent economic and fiscal impact analysis and independent underwriting analysis conducted by a third-party. Consultants were selected to conduct the analysis by the MEDC and the State Treasurer through a competitive RFP process. The University of Michigan Research Seminar in Quantitative Economic (RSQE) along with the W.E. Upjohn Institute for Employment Research performed an Economic and Fiscal Impact Assessment and SB Friedman Development Advisors conducted an underwriting analysis of the Project.  The reports were produced in response to the first TBP application.

Revised Date:
